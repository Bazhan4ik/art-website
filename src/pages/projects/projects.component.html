Valentyna

Untidiness

I used colored pencils and paper.

Still life aims to create a drawing of an exact copy of some objects that you see. The piece was intended to show my skills of drawing realistically with pencils.

I utilized Emphasis and Color to highlight the violin, my favorite musical instrument. I strategically barely used any color for any objects because they don't deserve to stand out when a violin is in the picture. Violin is, obviously, the emphasis by being the biggest and the best object of all the other.

Now that I'm a little better with pencils, I'm confident that next time I will do better with coloring.

Date: 4/18/2024

Fillmore mini

I used cyrillic and a metal toy car.

I painted on a car, trying to immitate a modert artist called William Morris. William Morris paints beautiful flower patterns.

I utilized principles of Pattern and Balance in this piece. The flowers repeated is pattern, equally the same two sides is balance.

I was aiming to make this bright, pretty, light, design that brings happiness and ease of mind. It turned out a little darker than expected, but still the result was not as bad as expected.
